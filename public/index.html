FAQ

Questions before you install

How does the popup actually work?

When you make or receive a call, Swipcall shows a popup on your screen - on top of whatever app you're using. It shows the caller's info, your schedule, and action buttons. It stays until you dismiss it. You never need to open the app.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your customer data stays on your phone. Nothing is sent to any server. When you back up, it goes to your own Google Drive, encrypted with your account.

Why does it need permissions?

Two core permissions: phone state (to detect calls) and overlay (to show the popup). Optional permissions for contacts matching and notifications. All data stays on your device.

I already use a caller ID app. Do I need this?

Caller ID tells you who's calling. Swipcall lets you do something about it - schedule a visit, send a WhatsApp, save the customer - without leaving the call. They solve different problems.

Does it work on iPhone?

No. The popup requires Android-specific permissions that iOS doesn't support. Android only.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Popup, customer list, and calendar all work without internet. Only WhatsApp and backup need a connection.

What happens after 20 customers?

Your data stays. You can still view and use everything. You just can't add new customers until the Pro plan launches. Nothing is ever deleted.
