Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Summary

Swipcall stores all your data locally on your phone. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to your personal data, customer data, call logs, or contacts.

What data Swipcall uses

Swipcall accesses phone call state and call log to detect incoming and outgoing calls and display the popup overlay. It accesses your contacts to show caller information. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to any server.

Data storage

All customer records, appointments, call history, and settings are stored in a local database on your Android device. No data is stored on external servers.

Backups

If you choose to enable backup, your data is encrypted using AES-256-GCM and uploaded to your personal Google Drive account. Only you can access this backup. Swipcall does not have access to your Google Drive or the encryption key.

Third-party services

Swipcall uses Firebase for crash reporting and basic anonymous analytics (app opens, feature usage counts). No personally identifiable information is included in these reports. No customer data, phone numbers, or call details are ever transmitted.

WhatsApp integration

When you tap "Send WhatsApp" in the popup, Swipcall opens the WhatsApp app with the customer's phone number using a standard Android deep link. No data passes through Swipcall's servers - it goes directly from your device to WhatsApp.

Permissions

Swipcall requests the following Android permissions:

READ_PHONE_STATE / READ_CALL_LOG: To detect when calls start and end, and to show caller information in the popup.

SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW: To display the floating popup overlay on top of other apps.

READ_CONTACTS: To match phone numbers with contact names.

POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To send callback reminders.

Data export and deletion

You can export all your data at any time from Settings. You can delete all data by uninstalling the app or using the "Delete all data" option in Settings. Deletion is permanent and irreversible.

Children's privacy

Swipcall is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 16.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
